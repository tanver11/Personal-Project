

1. **Overall Inventory Overview:**  
   - **Question:** Total inventory koto? (Total Current Stock)  
   - **Insight:** KPI cards diye total stock, average stock, aar total products count dekhte paro.  
   - **Visual:** KPI cards, donut/bar chart by category.

2. **Time Series Analysis:**  
   - **Question:** Inventory levels kibhabe time-er sathe change hocche?  
   - **Insight:** Daily/Monthly trend analysis kore seasonality ba sudden changes identify kora.  
   - **Visual:** Line chart with Date axis, slicers for month/year.

3. **Product Performance & Stock Levels:**  
   - **Question:** Kon product gulor current stock beshi/kom?  
   - **Insight:** Kon product stock reorder level er niche ache, ar kon product er stock consistently high ache.  

   - **Visual:** Bar chart comparing Current_Stock vs. Reorder_Level per product.

4. **Low Stock / Reorder Analysis:**  
   - **Question:** Koto product reorder er dorkar?  
   - **Insight:** Low stock (current_stock < reorder_level) product count, product category wise low stock distribution.  
   - **Visual:** Table with conditional formatting, bar chart highlighting low-stock items.

5. **Supplier Performance:**  
   - **Question:** Kon supplier er product gulo beshi low-stock hoy?  
   - **Insight:** Supplier-wise stock distribution, supplier jara consistently low stock er product supply kore tar analysis.  
   - **Visual:** Pie chart or bar chart grouping by Supplier.

6. **Warehouse Distribution:**  
   - **Question:** Kon warehouse e koto inventory ache?  
   - **Insight:** Warehouse wise inventory allocation aar performance, kon warehouse er stock variance beshi.  
   - **Visual:** Bar chart or tree map, interactive slicer by Warehouse.

7. **Category Breakdown:**  
   - **Question:** Electronics, Furniture, aar Stationery category te inventory distribution kemon?  
   - **Insight:** Category wise stock analysis, jekhane Electronics dominate korte pare ba kono category te stock shortage thakte pare.  
   - **Visual:** Stacked bar chart or donut chart by Category.

8. **Variance & Anomaly Detection:**  
   - **Question:** Kon product er stock level onek variation dekhachhe?  
   - **Insight:** Outliers ba anomalies detect kora jate unexpected fluctuations identify kora jay.  
   - **Visual:** Box plot or scatter plot.

9. **Trend Comparison:**  
   - **Question:** Time series e current stock aar reorder level er trend ek sathe kemon?  
   - **Insight:** Dual-axis chart e dekhao je kon month e reorder er gap beshi chhilo, jate proactive steps nite paro.  
   - **Visual:** Dual-axis line chart with Current_Stock aar Reorder_Level.

10. **Inventory Efficiency Metrics (Advanced):**  
    - **Question:** Kon product category te inventory management efficient? (jodi aro sales data thake)  
    - **Insight:** Inventory turnover ratio, stock to reorder ratio calculate kore efficiency measure kora jay.  
    - **Visual:** KPI cards, gauges, aar custom DAX calculations.

---

**Additional Interactivity Suggestions:**  
- **Slicers/Filters:** Date, Product, Category, Supplier, Warehouse diye dashboard ke interactive koro.  
- **Drill-down Features:** Product category theke specific product details e drill down korar option dao.  
- **Tooltips:** Hover korle detailed metrics ba insights show korar jonno tooltips add koro.

Ei question/insights gulo upor base kore tumi dashboard design korle, user der real-time inventory trends, potential stock issues, aar supplier/warehouse performance niye clear picture pawa jabe. Happy dashboard designing!

Key Insights from the Supply Chain Dashboard

1.Overall Inventory Overview:
Track total inventory, average stock, and product count using KPI cards and visualizations.

2.Time Series Analysis:
Identify seasonality and sudden inventory changes through daily/monthly trends.

3.Product Performance & Stock Levels:
Detect products with low stock or consistently high inventory for better stock management.

4.Low Stock / Reorder Analysis:
Highlight products below reorder level to ensure timely restocking.

5.Supplier Performance:
Analyze which suppliers frequently have low-stock products to assess reliability.

6.Warehouse Distribution:
Monitor inventory allocation across warehouses and identify stock variance.

7.Category Breakdown:
Evaluate inventory distribution by category (e.g., Electronics, Furniture) to spot imbalances.

8.Variance & Anomaly Detection:
Identify unexpected stock fluctuations and anomalies for proactive decision-making.

9.Trend Comparison:
Compare current stock vs. reorder level trends to optimize inventory planning.

10.Inventory Efficiency Metrics (Advanced):
Measure inventory turnover and stock efficiency if sales data is available.
